Our Work

Explore the innovative projects and initiatives developed by Swivel IT & Training Institute.

Grabbasket.com – E-Commerce Platform

Grabbasket is one of our proudest projects — an innovative E-Commerce platform built entirely in Tamil Nadu. It represents the next generation of online shopping with a focus on convenience, speed, and reliability.

Grabbasket combines three major service models into one powerful platform:

  • Hotel Delivery: Bringing your favorite local restaurants online with doorstep delivery.
  • 10-Minute Delivery: Quick delivery services like Blinkit and Zepto, offering lightning-fast groceries and essentials.
  • Food Aggregation: A restaurant and meal-ordering system similar to Zomato and Swiggy, integrated seamlessly into the same platform.

This 3-in-1 E-Commerce website is a major step toward redefining the online delivery ecosystem, merging convenience with cutting-edge technology. We are proud to say — Developed by our own Swivel IT Team!
